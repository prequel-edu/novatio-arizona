Arizona ESA Program

Arizona families pay nothing out of pocket

The state issues an Education Savings Account (ESA) voucher on your behalf. That money goes straight to Novatio. You never write a check.

How it actually works

The Arizona ESA is a state-issued voucher — not a reimbursement. You do not pay tuition and wait to get money back. The state deposits the funds directly into an account, and Novatio is paid from there. Your family pays $0 at any point in the process.

Step by step:
1
You apply for the ESA through the Arizona Department of Education. We walk you through every step.
2
ADE approves your account and deposits your ESA funds. This covers Novatio's full tuition.
3
Novatio is paid directly from your ESA. Any remaining balance is yours for other approved educational expenses.

ESA amounts vary by student and are administered by the Arizona Department of Education. All Arizona K–12 students qualify — no income test, no special requirements.

Universal eligibility. Unlike programs that require financial need or a disability, Arizona's ESA is open to every K–12 student in the state. All Novatio applicants are accepted.
