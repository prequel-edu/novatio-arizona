Arizona ESA Program

AZ Families — Tuition fully covered with ESA

The state issues an Education Savings Account voucher on your behalf. That money goes straight to Novatio. You never write a check to the school.

You never pay Novatio directly.

The Arizona ESA is a state-issued voucher — not a reimbursement. You do not pay tuition first and wait to get money back. The state deposits the funds directly, and Novatio is paid from there. Your family pays $0 at any point in the process.

1
You apply for the ESA through the Arizona Department of Education. We walk you through every step.
2
ADE approves your account and deposits your ESA funds. This covers Novatio's full tuition.
3
Novatio is paid directly from your ESA. Any remaining balance is yours for other approved educational expenses.
