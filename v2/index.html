Arizona ESA Program

AZ families: tuition fully covered with ESA

The state issues an Education Savings Account on your behalf. That money goes straight to Novatio. You never write a check to the school.

You never pay Novatio directly.

The Arizona ESA is a state-issued voucher, not a reimbursement. You do not pay tuition first and wait to get money back. The state deposits funds directly, and Novatio is paid from there. Your family pays $0 at every step.

1
You apply for the ESA through the Arizona Department of Education. We walk you through every step.
2
ADE approves your account and deposits your ESA funds. This covers Novatio's full tuition.
3
Novatio is paid directly from your ESA. Any remaining balance is yours for other approved educational expenses.

Common Questions

Everything you want to know.

Honest answers to the questions Arizona parents ask most.

Is Novatio really free for Arizona families?⌄
Yes, completely. Through Arizona's ESA (Education Savings Account) program, the state covers Novatio's full tuition. Every Arizona K–12 student qualifies, there's no income test, and all Novatio applicants are accepted. You never pay anything.
How does the Arizona ESA program work?⌄
The ESA is a state-funded voucher the Arizona Department of Education deposits on your behalf. It covers Novatio's tuition in full. You don't pay first and get reimbursed. Any remaining ESA balance is yours to use on other approved educational expenses like tutoring, curriculum, or therapy.
Who qualifies for the Arizona ESA?⌄
Every Arizona K–12 student qualifies. It's a universal program. There is no income requirement, no disability requirement, and no minimum academic standard. If your student lives in Arizona, they qualify.
How many hours a day does school take?⌄
Core academics take about 2 hours each morning. The full school day runs from 8 AM to 2 PM, with the remaining time for games, breaks, lunch, and afternoon clubs. There is no homework. Ever.
Is Novatio accredited?⌄
Yes. Novatio is accredited by Cognia, the leading accrediting organization for K–12 schools in the US and internationally. Cognia accreditation is recognized by colleges, universities, and employers worldwide.
Can my student work at their own pace?⌄
Yes, that's the entire point. Our AI platform adapts to each student's exact level in every subject simultaneously. A student can be at grade 3 in math and grade 7 in social studies, and the system adjusts automatically every day. No more being held back, no more getting left behind.
What if my child needs extra support or has an IEP?⌄
Novatio supports students with IEPs, 504 plans, and diverse learning needs. The AI adapts to every learning style, and live teachers are on-screen throughout the day for real-time guidance. Contact our admissions team to discuss your student's specific situation before applying.
How is Novatio different from other online schools in Arizona?⌄
Most online schools move the traditional model to a screen: same 6-hour day, same passive lectures, same homework. Novatio was built differently from the ground up. AI compresses academics into 2 focused hours, and the rest of the day is spent building real-world skills most schools never touch.
Do students actually socialize and make friends?⌄
Yes, Novatio is a real community. Students are on video together all day: morning games, lunch, and collaborative clubs. Many students say the friendships at Novatio are stronger than what they had at their previous school.
